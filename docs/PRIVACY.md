# Canvas Search - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 2026

---

## Summary

Canvas Search is a privacy-first Chrome extension. Here's what you need to know:

✅ **All data stays on your computer**
✅ **No data is sent to any server**
✅ **No tracking or analytics**
✅ **You can delete all data anytime**

---

## What Data We Collect

When you click "Re-scan Canvas", we collect:

| Data Type | Example | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Link titles | "Week 3 Lecture Slides" | Search indexing |
| Link URLs | `https://school.instructure.com/...` | Opening results |
| Module names | "Module 2: Cell Biology" | Search context |
| File names | "lecture_notes.pdf" | Search indexing |
| Content types | "pdf", "video", "assignment" | Filtering |

---

## What We DON'T Collect

❌ **Personal information** - No names, emails, or student IDs
❌ **Passwords** - Never accessed or stored
❌ **Grades** - Not collected
❌ **Assignment content** - Only titles, not actual content
❌ **Messages** - Private messages are never accessed
❌ **Browsing history** - Only Canvas pages you scan
❌ **Location data** - Not collected
❌ **Cookies** - Never accessed

---

## Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored **locally on your computer** using Chrome's Storage API.

| Storage Type | Location | Access |
|--------------|----------|--------|
| `chrome.storage.local` | Your computer only | This extension only |

**No cloud storage. No servers. No external databases.**

---

## How Data Is Used

Your data is used for **one purpose only**:

> To let you search your Canvas course content quickly.

We do NOT:
- Sell your data
- Share your data
- Analyze your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Send your data anywhere

---

## Data Retention

**Automatic**: Data is stored until you delete it.

**Manual deletion**: Click "Clear All Data" in the extension popup.

**On uninstall**: Chrome removes all extension data automatically.

---

## Third-Party Services

Canvas Search uses **zero** third-party services.

| Service | Used? | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Analytics | ❌ No | No tracking whatsoever |
| Advertising | ❌ No | No ads |
| Cloud storage | ❌ No | All local |
| External APIs | ❌ No | No network calls |

---

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

1. **View**: Search results show your indexed data
2. **Delete**: Click "Clear All Data" anytime
3. **Export**: Data is stored locally (accessible to you)
4. **Portability**: Data is in standard JSON format
